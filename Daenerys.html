Emilia Clarke

Daenerys Targaryen

Emilia Clarke is a very well-known English actress born in 23 October 1986 in London who was interested in acting since she was a child and appeared in some plays in the secondary school. Later, she went to study at the Drama Centre London, where she graduated in 2009. After that she appeared in a play by the Company of Angels, in two commercials for Samaritans, and in a University of London student’s short film. Her first appearance on television was in one episode of the British soap opera Doctors, after that being recognize as one of the UK Stars of Tomorrow by Screen International magazine for her role in Syfy’s film Triassic Attack (2010).

Receiving the role for Daenerys Taragaryen in the HBO series Game of Thrones helped her to gain both critical and popular acclaim. The role of Daenerys was originally played by Tamzin Merchant in the unaired pilot episode, but the role was recast for the actual series for undisclosed reasons.
